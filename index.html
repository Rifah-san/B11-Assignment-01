Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh aspires to foster a culture of innovation by investing in technology, research, and digital transformation. This goal aims to drive economic growth and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

The nation is committed to sustainable development by adopting renewable energy, reducing carbon emissions, and ensuring environmental preservation for future generations.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is enhancing its infrastructure with modern transportation networks, smart cities, and robust connectivity to support urbanization and economic expansion.

Empowerment

Empowering youth, women, and marginalized groups remains a priority, with initiatives focused on education, skill development, and entrepreneurship.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh has the opportunity to show the world a new model of development—one where we combine the power of technology, the wisdom of sustainability, and the strength of our people to create a nation free from poverty and inequality. This is our chance to build a Bangladesh 2.0 that inspires the future."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
